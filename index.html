GOOD FOOD & GREAT VIBES

The term "Rasassi" is derived from the 15th-century word "Rasassi" , which means to satisfy one's hunger, and "Rasassi" someone" means to satisfy someone with a delicious meal.

Our mission is to provide a unique and relaxing dining experience at a premier home-style dining restaurant, similar to dining at home.

Whatever the season, we have access to the very best suppliers who can provide us with the best quality meat, fish, and vegetables we use in our cooking.

So if you want to visit the best restaurant in Altrincham and satisfy your hunger, take a look at our menu now.
